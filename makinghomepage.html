The homepage will have the assignments for the students in the yearbook and photography club.

Identify and plan consistency into website pages

The template will be used for all the websites, so there will be a form of consistency between the subpages and the homepages, it will have the same format throughout the subpages.

What message is your website giving out overall?

Our main message that we are trying to convey is to give knowledge about when the yearbook photography team wants their assignments due, and what it requires.

We don't have a named theme (meaning that we didn't aim for a certain theme) but we tried to make the outlook of our website acceptable for both genders as males do not like a website completely filled pink flowers vice versa. We tried to make our theme extremely simple as the client wanted it to be, and he thinks the theme is perfect.
